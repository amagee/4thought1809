Coincraft

Practice & Project Management For Architects

Stay on budget, stay on schedule

CoinCraft is a tool that allows an architecture practice to easily plan their project fee proposals, manage budgets and profits, as well as schedule their resources. Combined with our time-tracking tools, architecture practice's can look at their history and create benchmarks for future project fee proposals. CoinCraft updates in real time and will alert you of any potential hazards, making sure your business stays profitable and maintains growth.

Simple Timesheets

CoinCraft provides a beautiful and simple to use interface for all you staff members to record their timesheets. Coupled with reporting tools you can find all the historic data you need for your architecture practice, whether it be for paying your staff, managing your costs or informing future fee proposals.

Schedule staff & see what they're working on.

CoinCraft's resourcing tool allows you to schedule staff to particular projects and let's you see what they're working on with your practice. You can quickly identify staff with too little or too much workload and manage the consequences of project movements.

You can use both timesheeting and resourcing tools together to compare your schedule versus your actuals and track whether you are on time and on budget with your projects.
